About Us

We are pleased to announce the formation of the South Asian Medical Society (SAMS) at The University of Manchester.

SAMS is founded on the principle of uniting medical students of South Asian origin, providing a professional and supportive atmosphere for networking, scholastic collaboration, and cultural celebration. We explicitly state that this society is an inclusive organization.

While our founding mission is focused on the South Asian community, membership and all activities are open to the entire student body. Our primary focus is academic enrichment. SAMS is dedicated to delivering high-yield, regular teaching sessions that diverge from conventional pedagogy.

Our objective is to furnish interactive, clinically-pertinent instruction that complements the core curriculum. This is delivered in conjunction with tailored academic support systems intended to ensure all students can achieve their full potential.

We look forward to offering weekly tutorials, formal mentorship pairings, and a durable community network to provide comprehensive support throughout your medical school career.
